Echo Nest Remix is the Internet Synthesizer that lets you make things with music and video. Remix has done the following: rearranged the syllables of a vocal line, walkenized and cowbellized hundreds of thousands of songs in a week, mixed up the hits of a drum beat and synced it to another loop, beat matched two songs, split apart DJ mixes by their individual tracks, made new kinds of video mashups, corrected sloppy drumming, synced a video to a song, transitioned between two covers of the same song, made a cat play piano, put a donk on it. We're making this available as an open source SDK for Python and JavaScript that can be easily installed on Mac OS X, Linux, or Windows. It accesses the Echo Nest Analyze API at http://developer.echonest.com and lets you play with the results to make new concoctions of audio and video. You'll need an Echo Nest API key to use this SDK: instructions are below.
